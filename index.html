About

Escrow crypto is a service that brings both the traditional online market and the modern online market together.

The purpose of escrow crypto is to make the buyer and seller comfortable with transactions in the modern online world.

escrow crypto is an intermediary party that facilitates payments from crypto like bitcoin & usdt to fiat currency or crypto to crypto.

Headquartered in the United Kingdom, Escrow Crypto and its operating subsidiaries provide online escrow services that facilitate and accelerate e-commerce by assuring a secure settlement.

Escrow Crypto has pioneered the process of online exchange services. The company has established itself as one of the leading providers of secure business and consumer transaction management on the Internet.

CRYPTO SWAPPING

Crypto swapping involves the following:

Whenever two participants decide to exchange their crypto for a certain reason, we can facilitate the transaction.

Participant A, located in Dubai, would like to exchange his Bitcoins to Cardano with participant B who is located in the United Kingdom.

Both parties sign an agreement, drawn up by themselves, with the terms and conditions. If the agreement is signed, both parties will send their crypto to Escrow Crypto and Escrow Crypto sends the bitcoins to customer B and the Cardano's to customer A.

MILESTONE TRANSACTIONS

A milestone transaction is when a payment is made in installments and the service is also delivered in installments.

Milestone transactions divide a transaction into multiple phases. Milestone transactions can be used for a transfer of goods, services, or a combination of both goods and services. For instance, let’s say you are hiring an influencer to do some promotion work for your brand. You can arrange the milestone transaction in such a way that you can release 50% of the payment when the photoshoot is complete, and the remaining 50% when the influencer places the content on his social media account.
